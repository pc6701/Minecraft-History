Introduction

Minecraft began as an indie game created by Swedish programmer Markus "Notch" Persson in 2009. Inspired by games like Infiniminer, it introduced a unique block-based world where players could build, explore, and survive. What started as a small passion project soon grew into one of the most influential games in history.

Become a Builder

Dive into an open world of building, crafting, and survival in Minecraft - the ultimate sandbox building simulation game. Gather resources, survive the night, and build an epic adventure one block at a time. Explore and craft your way through a completely open world where you can play with friends.

Build houses, castles, cities, or farms. Let your imagination come to life as you create your own world. Play Creative Mode with unlimited resources or Survival Mode where you’ll face danger and craft tools to thrive.

Features

  • Explore endless biomes and meet friendly (and hostile) mobs.
  • Access add-ons and content via the Minecraft Marketplace.
  • Customize gameplay with slash commands and mods.
  • Cross-platform multiplayer with friends on Bedrock Edition.
  • Private servers and Realms Plus subscription for shared worlds.

Multiplayer

Join free servers, compete in mini-games, and explore community worlds with thousands of other players. With Realms Plus, play with up to 10 friends across platforms and enjoy new Marketplace content monthly.

Legacy

Today, Minecraft is more than a game—it’s a cultural phenomenon. It is used in schools, inspires creativity worldwide, and continues to receive updates and community support.
